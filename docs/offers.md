# Foundations of Strength — Offer Development

## Hormozi Framework (Value Equation)
> Dream Outcome × Likelihood of Achievement
> ÷ Time Delay × Effort Required
>
> Maximize the top. Minimize the bottom.
> The guarantee should scare you a little. If it doesn't, it's not strong enough.

---

## Offer A — The 90-Day Foundation
**Tagline:** "Go from scattered to structured in 90 days or your money back."

**Best for:** Men who are ready to commit to a fixed container with a clear outcome.

**What's included:**
- Full strength assessment
- Custom 12-week program
- Weekly check-ins
- Video form review (unlimited submissions)
- Nutrition targets
- Direct messaging access

**Guarantee:** If your squat, hinge, or press doesn't measurably improve in 90 days → 30 more days free.

**Price range:** $1,200–$1,800 one-time

**Why it works:** Fixed container, clear outcome, low perceived risk. Easiest to sell from a landing page. Use this to build your first 5–10 case studies.

---

## Offer B — Ongoing Monthly Coaching
**Tagline:** "A real coach, a real plan, real results — or cancel anytime."

**Best for:** Men who want continuity without a large upfront commitment.

**What's included:**
- Monthly custom programming
- Weekly check-ins (call or message)
- Video form review
- Nutrition + recovery targets
- Program adjustments as life changes

**Guarantee:** First month — if it doesn't feel built for you specifically, full refund.

**Price range:** $350–$600/month

**Why it works:** Lower barrier to entry, recurring revenue, builds long-term relationship and results.

---

## Offer C — The Application-Only Intensive
**Tagline:** "For the guy who is serious. 6 months. Limited spots."

**Best for:** Higher-intent buyers. Premium positioning. After you have case studies from Offer A.

**What's included:**
- Full assessment + baseline strength testing
- 6-month periodized program
- Bi-weekly coaching calls
- Unlimited video review
- Nutrition framework
- Direct access to Christian

**Guarantee:** Hit the specific strength targets agreed on at the start — or coaching extends at no charge until you do.

**Price range:** $3,000–$5,000 for 6 months

**Why it works:** Application-only signals exclusivity. Guarantee is iron-clad but achievable. Premium clients expect premium access.

---

## Recommended Launch Sequence

1. **Start with Offer A** — fixed price, strong guarantee, easy to explain on a landing page
2. **Collect 5–10 results/testimonials** from Offer A clients
3. **Open Offer C** as the premium tier — use Offer A results as proof
4. **Offer B** runs in the background as a lower-barrier option for people not ready to commit to a fixed container
